NATURE OF OFFENCE: Assault / Grievous Hurt (IPC Section 325)

BRIEF FACTS:
On 02-Dec-2025 at 20:45 hours, complainant Kavitha Reddy was returning from her
office at ITPL when she was accosted by two males near the BMTC bus stop on
Whitefield Main Road. The accused demanded her mobile phone and handbag. When she
resisted, one accused struck her on the shoulder with an iron rod causing a fracture
to the left clavicle. Complainant raised an alarm; the accused fled towards the
Whitefield railway station.

VICTIM MEDICAL REPORT:
Treated at Manipal Hospital Whitefield. Injury certificate confirms fracture of
left clavicle. Estimated recovery: 6-8 weeks.

MODUS OPERANDI:
Two accused, males aged approximately 20-25 years. One was wearing a red hoodie.
They targeted individuals near poorly lit bus stops. This is the third such incident
near Whitefield bus stops in December 2025.

ACCUSED DESCRIPTION:
Accused 1: Male, ~5'8", thin build, red hoodie, jeans
Accused 2: Male, ~5'6", medium build, grey shirt, carried iron rod

STATUS: Charge Sheeted
