NATURE OF OFFENCE: Online Fraud / Cyber Crime (IT Act Section 66C, 66D; IPC 420)

BRIEF FACTS:
On 18-Dec-2025, complainant Ramesh Iyer received a phone call from an individual
claiming to be from the "KYC Verification Department of State Bank of India." The
caller stated his account would be blocked unless he verified his OTP. Complainant,
believing the call to be genuine, shared the OTP. Within minutes, Rs. 87,500 was
debited from his savings account via NEFT transfer to an account in Rajasthan.
