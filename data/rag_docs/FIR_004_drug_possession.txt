FIRST INFORMATION REPORT
FIR Number: HSR/008/2026
Date: 2026-01-05
Police Station: HSR Layout Police Station, Bengaluru Urban District

REPORTING OFFICER: Inspector Arun Desai (Badge: 3812)

NATURE OF OFFENCE: Drug Possession and Trafficking (NDPS Act Sections 20, 22, 29)

BRIEF FACTS:
On 04-Jan-2026, acting on credible intelligence, Inspector Arun Desai along with
a team of 4 officers conducted a search operation near the BTM Layout lake-side
park at 23:00 hours. Two individuals were found in suspicious circumstances near
the park boundary.

Upon search of the accused persons and their vehicle (Maruti Suzuki Swift,
KA-01-MX-9876), the following contraband was seized:

SEIZURE:
1. Ganja (Cannabis): 2.3 kg (packed in 15 polythene packets)
2. MDMA tablets: 47 tablets (approximately 12g)
3. Cash: Rs. 45,000 (suspected drug sale proceeds)
4. Two mobile phones (seized for forensic analysis)

ACCUSED:
1. Ravi Kumar, 28 years, resident of BTM Layout 2nd Stage
2. Deepak Shetty, 31 years, resident of Jayanagar 4th Block

MODUS OPERANDI:
Accused operated from the park at night. They had a network of young buyers
from nearby colleges and tech parks. Ravi Kumar is alleged to be the supplier;
Deepak Shetty is the distributor.

STATUS: Charge Sheeted - Both accused remanded to judicial custody
SECTIONS INVOKED: NDPS Act 20(b)(ii)(C), 22(c), 29; IPC 34
